Orchestration

Context

This was the final project of my orchestration course. The goal of the project was to orchestrate Claude Debussy's La Fulle aux Cheveux de Lin or The Girl with the Flaxen Hair. This was a solo project.

Result:

The original work is for solo piano. The orchestration includes strings, woodwinds (3 kinds of flutes), brass and harp.

La Fille aux Cheveux de Lin
