Choral Arranging

For this course, write a choral arrangement of any other work or tune.

There were three requirements for the sum of the works:

One had to have accompaniment. One had to be written for SSA. Two different styles of music needed to be represented.

"Black is the Color" is scored for SSA and has accompaniment.
"Bright is the Ring of Words" is a classical work.
"Sound of Silence" is a pop work.

Black is the Color - John Jacob Niles
Bright is the Ring of Words - R. Vaughan Williams
Sound of Silence - Simon and Garfunkel
